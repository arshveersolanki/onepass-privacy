# OnePass EDU Support

OnePass EDU is the school operations app for **Imagine International Academy of North Texas**. Accounts are provisioned by your school administrator under the institutional account model — there is no self-registration in the app.

## How to get help

| What you need | Where to go |
|---|---|
| Forgot your password / locked out | Contact your school administrator |
| Add or remove a guardian on a family | Contact your school administrator |
| Update student information (grade, classroom, etc.) | Contact your school administrator |
| Add or remove registered vehicles | In-app: **Profile → Vehicles → +** |
| Report a bug or feature request | Email **upender.solanki@novatiosolutions.com** |
| Privacy or data deletion request | Email **privacy@unifiedcampussystems.com** or contact your school administrator |

## Common questions

**I forgot my password.**
Parents log in with email + a one-time code (OTP) — no password to remember. If you don't receive the OTP, ask your school administrator to confirm the email on file.

Staff log in with the email + password your administrator gave you. If forgotten, the administrator can reset it for you.

**My kid is missing from my account.**
The school provisions student records and links them to your family. If a student is missing or in the wrong family, contact your school administrator.

**The app says "Guardian not found."**
The email you entered doesn't match what the school has on file. Confirm with your school administrator that they have the correct email for you.

**I'd like to delete my account.**
OnePass EDU uses an institutional account model — your account was created by the school under the **Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)**. To request deletion, email **privacy@unifiedcampussystems.com** or contact your school administrator. Requests are processed within 30 days.
