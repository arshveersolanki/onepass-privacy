# OnePass EDU Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 26, 2026

## 1. Overview

OnePass EDU ("the App") is a school operations platform used by Imagine International Academy of North Texas ("the School") and its authorized staff and guardians for student dismissal, attendance, hall-pass management, visitor management, and emergency communication. The App is provided by Unified Campus Systems LLC ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## 2. Institutional Account Model

OnePass EDU uses an **institutional account model**. The School provisions accounts for staff and guardians through its internal administration tools. **Users do not create accounts through the App.** All user information is initially provided to us by the School under its agreements with parents and staff.

To request access, modification, or deletion of your account or data, contact your School administrator or email **privacy@unifiedcampussystems.com**.

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the School's dismissal, attendance, safety, hall-pass, and visitor-management functions.

**Staff accounts:**

- Name, school-issued email, role (administrator, teacher, nurse, security, helper, etc.)
- Authentication credentials (stored only as bcrypt password hashes; we never see the plaintext password)

**Guardian accounts:**

- Name, email, phone number
- Family identifier and relationship to enrolled students

**Student records (provided by the School):**

- Name, grade, IB programme classification (PYP / MYP / DP)
- Attendance status and timestamps
- Hall-pass history and accommodations (e.g., extra time for medical needs, hall-pass blocks)
- Dismissal queue position and pickup status

**Operational data:**

- Registered vehicles (license plate, optional description, optional temporary-vehicle expiration)
- Visitor records (name, license plate, photo when collected by school staff)
- Emergency event records (type, level, drill flag, initiating staff member, timestamp)
- Messages exchanged via the in-app assistant feature, if used

We do **not** collect:

- Location data
- Financial or payment information
- Health or fitness data
- Browsing or search history outside the App
- Contacts from your device
- Advertising identifiers

We do **not** track users across other companies' apps or websites.

## 4. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely for the operational functions of the School, including:

- Authenticating and authorizing users
- Coordinating student dismissal and parent/guardian pickup
- Recording attendance and hall-pass activity
- Communicating emergencies, drills, and safety alerts
- Managing on-campus visitors and registered vehicles

We do not use information for advertising, profiling, behavioral analytics outside the App's functional needs, or sale to third parties.

## 5. Children's Data (FERPA / COPPA)

Student records in OnePass EDU are educational records covered by the **Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)**. The School is the controller of these records; Unified Campus Systems LLC processes them on the School's behalf under a FERPA-compliant data processing agreement. Parents may exercise their FERPA rights (review, amend, request deletion of student records) by contacting the School administrator.

The App is not directed at children under 13 as the primary user. Children's records are accessed only by their school-authorized guardians and school staff. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 through the App.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active records** are retained for the duration of the student's enrollment at the School plus a reasonable archival period required by Texas state record-keeping rules.
- **Account records** for departed staff and graduated families are retained for the period required by School policy and applicable law.
- **Deletion requests** processed under FERPA or upon individual request are completed within 30 days and confirmed by email.

## 7. Data Security

- Authentication tokens are issued as short-lived JSON Web Tokens.
- Passwords are stored only as bcrypt hashes; we never see plaintext passwords.
- Backend communications use the encryption capabilities of the operating system's networking stack.
- Access is gated by role-based authorization and the School's internal admin controls.

## 8. Third Parties

OnePass EDU's backend is operated by Unified Campus Systems LLC. We do not share user data with third-party advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers. We use system-level infrastructure providers (e.g., DigitalOcean for hosting, Apple Push Notification service for push delivery) under standard service agreements; these providers do not access user content beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- Access the data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact your School administrator or **privacy@unifiedcampussystems.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. Changes will be reflected by an updated effective date and announced in-App where significant.
